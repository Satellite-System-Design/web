Mission

Space systems for everyone.

We are building practical satellite system knowledge, tools, and prototypes that make space technology easier to understand, design, test, and use. The goal is simple: lower the barrier from idea to mission-ready engineering.

Project + Product + Service

Solving advanced space challenges through innovation.

We connect research projects, practical products, and focused engineering services to address current market needs in satellite systems, communication, computing, and mission design.

Systems

Innovative modular designs for real space problems.

Our systems approach emphasizes modular architectures, reusable subsystem patterns, and adaptable space platforms that can be composed to solve advanced mission, payload, and operations challenges.
